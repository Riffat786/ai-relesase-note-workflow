# Release 2025.8

## New Features

**Enhance Your Account Security with Multi-Factor Authentication**

You can now enable multi-factor authentication to protect your account with an extra layer of security. Email verification adds an additional safeguard to your login process, helping keep your data secure.

**Export Dashboard Reports to PDF**

You can now export your dashboard reports directly to PDF format. This makes it easy to share insights, create documentation, and maintain records for compliance purposes.

## Enhancements

**Faster Search Experience**

We've optimized our search functionality across the platform to deliver results more quickly. You'll now experience faster response times when searching for information throughout your portal.

## Bug Fixes

**Password Reset Email Reliability**

We've resolved an issue that prevented password reset emails from being reliably delivered to some users. Password recovery emails are now consistently delivered to your inbox.

**Notification Accuracy**

We've fixed a bug that was causing duplicate notifications to appear in your account. You'll now receive notifications as intended without any duplicates.
